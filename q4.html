Comparison of .png and .jpg

Ways that .pngs are superior to .jpgs:

  • Transparency: PNG allows a smoother blend with different background, that is because it supports transparency while JPEG do not.
  • Edit: PNG can be double saved and edited how many time you want without the quality loss.
  • Color Scope: PNG is more acceptable for images and variables because it supports the broader range of color..

Ways that .pngs are inferior to .jpgs:

  • Lack of uniformity: PNG is not supported world wide as JPG, which can cause some issues. Which could have limits too.
  • Size File: PNG files are too big so it can causes glitches or even an impact that makes it load multiple times.
